Company Overview

Breaking Movie-Dining Boundaries

Our Story

Founded in 2023, FlickNibble House pioneered the 'Cinema + Dining' fusion experience in China. We break traditional movie-watching boundaries by delivering fresh, high-quality food directly to your seat, creating immersive entertainment moments that combine film and flavor.

In 2024, we expanded our vision to international travel, specifically targeting Chinese couples aged 18-35 who seek romantic, experience-driven adventures. Our Japan tour packages integrate our signature cinema-dining concept with Japan's most romantic destinations.
